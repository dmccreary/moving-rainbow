# Mascot Image Prompts for Moving Rainbow Textbook

## Base Character Description

Pixel is a cheerful educational mascot for a children's MicroPython and LED programming textbook called "Moving Rainbow." Pixel is a friendly anthropomorphic WS2812B addressable LED with a perfectly circular body. The body is primarily matte charcoal-black, representing the LED package, surrounded by a glowing rainbow ring that smoothly transitions through red, orange, yellow, green, cyan, blue, and violet. In the center of the body is a stylized WS2812B LED package showing the three visible RGB LED chips (red, green, and blue) inside a white square housing.

Pixel has large expressive cartoon eyes with bright reflections, thick friendly eyebrows, and a wide joyful smile showing enthusiasm and curiosity. The eyes should feel welcoming and encouraging to 9-year-old children learning to code. Pixel has flexible cartoon arms ending in oversized white-glove hands and sturdy cartoon legs ending in colorful sneakers inspired by rainbow colors.

Style: modern flat-vector mascot design with subtle depth and soft shading, clean outlines, highly readable at small sizes, suitable for educational textbooks, websites, classroom posters, stickers, and lesson callouts. Child-friendly and professional. No text. No props. No background elements.

Background: fully transparent PNG. No white background, no black background, no gradients, no shadows extending beyond the character, and no checkered pattern. Character only, centered in the image. The entire mascot must fit within the canvas with a small transparent margin around all sides.
