# NeoPixel Train Graphic Novel Story

!!! prompt
    I would like you to use the /story-generator to generate a fun story about the "NeoPixel Train".  In this graphic novel story, we will describe how a NeoPixel strip works using the metaphor of a train going down a track.  First, the train leaves the station with a number of train cars that match the number of pixels.  

    Each time the train passes through a neopixel a car is taken off the track.  That car has three passengers: red, green and blue pixel characters like in the @docs/img/mascot/character-sheet.md-  Each of the three passengers carries 8 bits.  They put those 8 bits into the neopixel chip and POOF!  
    The Red, Green and Blue LEDs get the right values.  

    Start out with a single pattern of 12 pixels and 12 different colors of the rainbow on the LED strip.  End with the last car arriving at the last 12th pixel on the strip.  The train is finally empty.  Make the story fun and entertaining for for junior-high level students.
